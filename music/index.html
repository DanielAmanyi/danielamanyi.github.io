Daniel Amanyi is an Audio Engineer, Music Producer, and founder of SupersoniQ Audio — an independent audio technology and entertainment platform operating across music production, sound design, acoustic engineering, and professional audio solutions.

Through SupersoniQ Audio, he combines creativity with technical precision to deliver immersive sonic experiences for artists, businesses, and media productions across Africa and internationally — with credits including Al Jazeera, MOFA Taiwan, Multichoice, PlusTV Africa and News Central TV.
