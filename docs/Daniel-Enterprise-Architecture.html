The Most Important
Relationship

                  TITAN
                     │
                     │ produces
                     ▼
          Engineering Evidence
                     │
                     │ feeds
                     ▼
      Professional Identity Doctrine
                     │
                     │ publishes
                     ▼
              Public Website
                     │
                     │ distributes
                     ▼
             LinkedIn / GitHub
                     │
                     │ attracts
                     ▼
               Opportunities

Earlier this year, the hierarchy was:

Resume
  ↓
Projects
  ↓
Job

Today, the hierarchy is:

Research
  ↓
Evidence
  ↓
Identity
  ↓
Career

Career becomes an output.
