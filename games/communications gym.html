      About Comm·Gym

      Project Objective

      A simple, personal training application that helps an engineer whose technical ability exceeds their communication ability close that gap through deliberate practice.

      This is not a learning platform, LMS, course, RPG, or interview simulator product. It's a focused daily practice tool: click one button, get a realistic engineering communication prompt, respond by voice or text, get structured evaluation and actionable feedback, and the session is stored automatically. Over time, the data makes measurable progress visible.

      The goal is to make engineering communication automatic through repetition and objective feedback — the same way coding proficiency comes from writing code repeatedly.

      Every feature answers one question: does this increase the quality or quantity of deliberate practice? If not, it doesn't belong.

      Design Principles

      • One session takes less than 10 minutes.
      • One click starts practice.
      • No unnecessary navigation.
      • No gamification in v0.1.
      • Analytics matter more than achievements.
      • Feedback matters more than scores.
      • Consistency matters more than complexity.
      • Every session contributes to long-term progress tracking.

      Anti-Goals

      This is deliberately not:

      • A language learning app
      • A public speaking coach
      • An LMS or online course
      • A social platform
      • A multiplayer interview simulator
      • An RPG with levels, worlds, or achievements
      • A chatbot that teaches theory
      You arrive, train one specific communication skill, receive objective coaching, leave, and return tomorrow slightly better than yesterday.
